About

My name is Steven Gardner and I own a meme page that I hope will be successful one day.
I want to pursue an education in electronics, either Web Development or Computer Graphics.
I made this website to pass time, and because I could. It is a simple website that I added
onto over the year and has grown great potential.

When I first made this website it was made up of just colorless text, no fonts, and no background.
Now it has as much aesthetic as the 90's, which is really what i was going for. I want this website
to have as much appeal as possible in order to grow a good reputation for the things im capable of.
when friends and family ask what I can do I want to show them this website to prove that in my down
time i'm capable of making something amazing with alot of character.

I also run a not so popular meme page with a current following of 78, but its slowly growing and im
happy with it. My memes are not about being popular, I find it fun to make something that brings joy
to someone with a simple image or short video.
